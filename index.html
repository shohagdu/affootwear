100% Export Oriented Leather Footwear and Goods Manufacturer in Bangladesh

Alliance Leather Goods and Footwear Ltd. is a 100% export-oriented leather footwear and goods manufacturing unit. ALGFL has started in 2013 January with the state of art machineries from Europe, Taiwan and Japan. We comply international standard, heavy-duty backup generators and warehousing facilities. ALGFL maintain high standard management system and corporate strategy combined with a team of well-experienced technicians from China, India & Bangladesh. ALGFL is utilizing its in house facilities to achieve and maintain the optimum quality standard and delivery in the shortest time possible for the minimum inventory level of buyers.

A STANDARD OF EXCELLENCE

Alliance Leather Goods and Footwear Ltd. follows a meticulous handmade process for each of its products that bases on high quality standards and deep-rooted expertise. The structures of ALGFL’s leather footwear & bags vary between nice grain, fine soffione optic and texture, and are treated very carefully during the manufacturing process. The pleasant touch and odor of genuine leather in the final product are evidence for this brand’s promise!
