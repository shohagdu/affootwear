RESEARCH & DEVELOPMENT

Expertise

With our broad experience in a Wide Range of Bags related products for several European mid to high-end brands, we understand well your expectations with respect to quality, outlook and compliance.

Design Services

Accompanying our customers is mainly about understanding their needs and purposes. Taking into account our price point, market needs, intentions and functional requirements, our designer will submit a creative selection of material qualities and colors as well as professional drawings for your review.

Seamless Communication And Responsiveness

We make a point at quick, easy and honest communication. Our teams are dedicated to customer satisfaction and will work hand in hand with you all along the development process. Sampling usually takes 3 days to 15 days depending on your product while development from scratch involving design decisions is a longer process.

CONFIDENTIALITY

Because we are working with major brands, we know how confidentiality can be decisive to a smooth and successful access to market of new products line. Carrying our own brand, we are all the more sensitive to IP protection.

QUALITY ASSURANCE: YOUR STANDARDS ARE OURS

Our Quality Control staff is following up on your order from the first stitching line through to final packing:


Nnitial inspection: check cut pieces for size and general appearance, check leather panels with logo (print, embroidery, emboss). Logo panels are checked 100%

nitial inspection: check cut pieces for size and general appearance, check leather panels with logo (print, embroidery, emboss). Logo panels are checked 100%

Final inspection: control all finished goods (100% check) on appearance, conformity and strength, check packing conformity While some customers fully rely on our in-house quality controllers, some may nominate third party quality controllers.
